How To Use Bottle Trade

BottleTrade is a social network of craft beer enthusiasts, collectors and traders that allows users to create profiles, upload their bottles into a Cyber Cellar and interact with others to establish trades amongst each other. We created BottleTrade with the intention of bridging the gap between traders across the entire country and world so that others can have the opportunity to try beverages that are not available in certain markets due to distribution and production limitations. We encourage you to watch our videos below to see how the BottleTrade network works. Happy Trading!
